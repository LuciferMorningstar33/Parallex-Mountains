Unique And Adventurous Tours

North Dolomites Tour

Embark on an unforgettable day trip through the Northern Dolomites, where you'll traverse picturesque landscapes and charming villages. Begin your journey in the idyllic setting of Gardena, known for its breathtaking vistas and quaint Alpine atmosphere. From there, venture to the chic resort town of Cortina d'Ampezzo, explore the cultural richness of Badia, and conclude your adventure in the enchanting village of Dobbiaco, where history and natural beauty converge in perfect harmony.

Book Now

South Dolomites Tour

Experience a captivating day trip through the Southern Dolomites, beginning in the delightful city of Bolzano, where the fusion of Austrian and Italian cultures awaits exploration. Traverse breathtaking landscapes to reach Carezza, home to the renowned emerald-green lake framed by majestic peaks. Conclude your journey in the picturesque settings of Vigo de Fassa and the Fassa Valley, culminating in the quaint village of Canazei, promising a harmonious blend of adventure and tranquility amidst nature's grandeur.
